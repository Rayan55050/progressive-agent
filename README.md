## 🛠️ Installation and Setup

### Step 3: Configure Telegram Bot

progressive-agent works as a bot inside Telegram. You need to create a bot account and get a token:

- Open Telegram and search for **BotFather**.  
- Start a chat and type `/newbot`.  
- Follow instructions to give your bot a name and username.  
- Once created, BotFather will show a **token**. Save this token.  

### Step 4: Add Your Bot Token

- After installation, open the progressive-agent app.  
- You will see a field to enter your Telegram bot token. Paste the token here.  
- Click **Save** or **Connect**.  

The app will connect to Telegram servers and start running your personal AI agent.
