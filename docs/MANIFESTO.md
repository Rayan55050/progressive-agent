# Manifesto

## AI assistants shouldn't be locked behind corporate walls

The technology exists for everyone to have a personal AI assistant that manages email, tracks finances, monitors interests, and handles daily tasks. But today, this power is gatekept by platforms that charge subscriptions, harvest data, and limit functionality to what serves their business model.

We reject that model.

## Your data is yours. Your assistant is yours.

When an AI reads your email, manages your files, and knows your financial situation — that data must stay under your control. Not on someone else's servers. Not training someone else's models. Not subject to someone else's terms of service that change without notice.

Progressive Agent runs on your machine. Memory is a SQLite file on your disk. There is no cloud. There is no telemetry. There is no "we may share your data with partners." You own every byte.

## Open source is the way

Trust requires transparency. When software has access to your most personal data, you need the ability to audit every line of code. Open source isn't just a license — it's a commitment to building in public, accepting scrutiny, and empowering users to fork, modify, and improve.

We build in the open. Every tool, every skill, every design decision — documented and available. Contributions are welcome. Forks are encouraged. The goal isn't to build a product — it's to build a movement toward AI that serves people, not platforms.

## What we're building

A personal AI assistant that actually assists. Not a chatbot that generates text — an agent that takes action. One that remembers what you told it last week, monitors things you care about while you sleep, sends notifications when something matters, and executes tasks without being micromanaged.

One that runs on hardware you control, speaks your language, and works for you — not for an ad platform, not for a data broker, not for a quarterly earnings report.

The future of AI assistants is open, private, and personal.

We're building it. Join us.
