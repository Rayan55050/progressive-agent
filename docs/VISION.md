# Vision

## Everyone deserves a personal AI that works for them

AI assistants today are locked behind corporate platforms, harvesting your data, limiting what you can do, and charging you for the privilege of being a product.

We believe in a different path.

**Progressive Agent** is a personal AI assistant that you own, run on your hardware, and control completely. It's not a chatbot that generates text — it's a real assistant that takes action. It reads your email, tracks your finances, monitors your interests, manages your files, and does the work so you don't have to.

## Core beliefs

**Privacy is non-negotiable.** Your conversations, your memory, your data — all of it stays on your machine. No cloud databases. No telemetry. No "improving our models with your data." SQLite on your disk, period.

**Open source is the only way.** When your AI assistant has access to your files, email, and finances, you need to be able to read every line of code it runs. Closed-source assistants ask for trust they haven't earned.

**Self-hosted means self-sovereign.** No vendor can shut down your assistant, change the pricing, or alter its behavior. You run it. You control it. It works for you.

**Action over conversation.** A real assistant doesn't just talk — it does. It calls APIs, writes files, sends emails, and monitors the world on your behalf. Every feature we build is measured by one question: does it save the owner time?

## Where we're going

A world where setting up a personal AI assistant is as simple as installing an app. Where communities share tools and skills freely. Where your AI grows with you, remembers what matters, and acts on your behalf — privately, reliably, and without asking permission from anyone.

The path forward:
- **Short term:** A rock-solid personal assistant for power users
- **Medium term:** A plugin ecosystem where anyone can extend capabilities
- **Long term:** Multi-agent systems that handle complex, long-running goals autonomously

Not a chatbot. A real assistant.
